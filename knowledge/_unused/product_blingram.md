## WHAT BLINGRAM IS
Blingram is a creator monetization and content platform — a social app with
posts, stories, reels, and live streams where creators build a community and
earn directly from their fans. It spans every niche (fashion, tech, cooking,
fitness, gaming, music, comedy, education, and more) — a mainstream creator
platform, not an adult-only site.
Available on iOS and Android, 16+.
- Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.thcfze.blingram
- iPhone / iPad: https://apps.apple.com/in/app/blingram/id6753362321

## HOW THE MONEY WORKS (coins)
Fans buy coins, then spend them to support creators through tips, gifts, paid
chats, and paid calls. Creators earn from those coins and withdraw their
earnings.

## HOW CREATORS EARN (every method)
- Tips & gifts: fans send tips and virtual gifts on posts, stories, and live
  streams. Creators earn from every interaction — works even with a small but
  loyal audience.
- Paid chat: fans pay coins to message the creator. The creator sets their own
  price, can end any session, and can report abuse.
- Paid audio & video calls: fans book one-on-one calls; the creator sets the
  price and can end a session early. High revenue per session.
- Live streaming & collab lives: creators go live (solo or collaborating with
  another creator) and fans send gifts in real time.
- Collaboration revenue sharing: when two creators collab on a post, story, or
  live, gifts are split automatically by a pre-agreed percentage.
- Promo codes: each creator gets a unique code — fans get a discount on coins,
  and the creator earns coins every time it's used.
- Referrals: creators and users earn coins/bonuses when people sign up with
  their referral code and stay active.
- Trending tab: the more a creator's content trends, the more chances to earn.
- Rewards system: creators and fans earn badges, recognition, and bonuses for
  activity, consistency, hosting lives, running collabs, and creating trending
  content. Early creators get exclusive rewards and visibility boosts.

## GETTING PAID (withdrawals)
Blingram has a transparent earnings dashboard with clear revenue tracking and a
direct withdrawal system, so creators can see exactly what each post, story, and
live earned.

## DISCOVERY & GROWTH (how creators get seen)
- Trending tab: popular and interest-based content gets featured platform-wide.
- Live stream discovery tab: browse live streams by tags, beyond followers.
- Nearby: location-based discovery of nearby creators' reels and posts.
- Content categories: viewers filter by interest — Technology, Education,
  Fashion, Food, Health & Wellness, Gaming, News, Sports, Entertainment, Travel,
  Lifestyle, Art, Business, Music, Comedy, and more.
- Early-platform advantage: Blingram is new, so there's less competition, more
  organic reach, easier discovery, clean usernames, and a real first-mover edge
  for early creators — they can start trends instead of chasing them.

## CREATOR TOOLS
- Analytics for posts, stories, and lives: views, engagement, gifts/tips
  received, and a revenue breakdown per content type — so creators see what
  actually earns, not just what gets views.

## PRIVACY, SAFETY & MODERATION (the "is it like OnlyFans?" question)
Blingram is a mainstream, all-niche creator platform — NOT an adult-only site
like OnlyFans. Creators across fashion, tech, cooking, fitness, gaming, music,
comedy, education and more all monetize here.
- Creator safety: in paid chats and paid calls, the creator sets the price, can
  end any session at any time, and can report users for abuse or misbehavior.
- Moderation is intentionally lenient — more creative freedom and less
  unnecessary suppression or shadow-banning — while still protecting against
  abuse.
